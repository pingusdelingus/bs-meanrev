Black-Scholes Formula

\[ C(S,t) = SN(d_1) - Ke^{-rT}N(d_2) \] \[ d_1 = \frac{\ln(S/K) + (r + \sigma^2/2)T}{\sigma\sqrt{T}}, \quad d_2 = d_1 - \sigma\sqrt{T} \] \[ \text{where } N(x) \text{ is the cumulative normal distribution function} \]
